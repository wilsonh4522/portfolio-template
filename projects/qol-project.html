Quality of Life Project

In the first semester of 12th grade we had a reasearch class called "Participation in Government". In this class we identify a public policy issue that affects your neighborhood and community and do something to address it.

After getting inspired by the cybersecurity couse I did in Columbia Pre-College program, I decieded to do cybersecurity awarness because I realized that now many people are aware of the dangers in the internet.

  • In this class we had to do 8 annotated bibliographies where we go through and annotate artciles that relate to my topic and contact an expert reltated to my project.
  • We then had to take the 8 annotated bibliographies and expert's response and use it in our background paper as sources.
  • After that we had to conduct a surve to 125 people to get their demographic, behavioral, knowledge, and attitude towards cybersecurity awarness.
  • I used the data from the survey to then do my research and analysis paper. In this paper I analyzed the data and made conclusions about them.
